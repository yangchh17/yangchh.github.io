About

GIS professional with 6+ years of experience designing and automating geospatial data workflows. I operate as a one-person GIS department — building data structures, managing spatial datasets, and maintaining the broader technical system.

My work sits at the intersection of spatial data engineering and applied analysis. I’m interested in building systems that scale — not just producing outputs, but designing the infrastructure that makes good spatial decisions repeatable.

MSc in Geomatics for Environmental Management (UBC). BSc in Environmental Science with GIS Certificate (SFU). Currently a G.I.T. Applicant and drone pilot (basic).
